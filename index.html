Welcome to PAWU!

Patient advocates of the world, unite!

PAWU is

Patients Advocates Worldwide United

We are a loose group of patients advocates. No legal structure. Minimal costs. Maximum efficiency. Everyone interested by the subject can join.

Our only goal is to facilitate the communication between patient advocates. That’s it.

And we want to be global, so advocates from various countries can learn from each other.

There are many patients associations, groups, initiatives. They are all our friends. We don’t compete. We collaborate.
